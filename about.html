Our Story

Born from a need,
built with purpose.

WOLA was founded at the University of Nottingham in 2023 by Alinda Mutabazi, who recognised a gap: young African women in the diaspora and on the continent had ambition, intelligence, and drive â€” but lacked a structured home to channel it.

What began as a conversation became a movement. Within its first year, WOLA expanded to four countries, won Newcomer of the Year at the University of Nottingham, and built a community of hundreds of women committed to leading Africa's future from within.

Today, WOLA operates across the UK, Canada, Wales, and Uganda â€” with more chapters in development. Every programme, event, and initiative is designed with one goal: equipping African women to lead.
